About

As an illustrator, I am a student of MA Illustration in Edinburgh College of Art at the University of Edinburgh and I hold a BA in Online Journalism and New Media from Xi'an International Studies University. My projects span various areas, including children's picture book, pop-up book, game book, sculptural installation, mixed-media installation, concept illustration, and window display. I like to tell stories through narration and metaphor.
